About Me

Hello! My name is Akinyemi Borisade but, I go by Yemi to make it easier. I was born in Jacksonville, FL and happen to be the youngest of my 3 siblings. I would eventually graduate high school and attend the University of North Florida to obtain my Bachelor's in Criminal Justice. While obtaining my bachelor's degree, I met my compass, soon to be wife. Soon after that I obtained my Master's in Criminal Justice and became a police officer.

My wife and I went on to create a family that now consist of two rambunctious boys. The oldest is 6years old and is already promising to become a dentist while my youngest son is 2 years old and living a car free life.

What started as a dream job of policing turned into a dream differed as it became a career that was no longer for me. This brought me to the wonderful and limitless world of software development and engineering. I believe this is a field where I can truly show and devlop my talents further while aiding company practices.
